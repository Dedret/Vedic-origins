Shipping Policy

Fast & Secure Delivery Across India

Free Shipping on prepaid orders above ₹1000!

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship across India to all serviceable pin codes. During checkout, you can verify if your area is serviceable by entering your pin code.

2. Processing Time

  • Order Processing: 1-2 business days
  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM IST on business days are typically processed the same day
  • Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your order is dispatched with tracking details

3. Delivery Time

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Tier 2 Cities: 4-6 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location, weather conditions, and courier availability.

4. Shipping Charges

  • Prepaid Orders:
    • Free shipping on orders above ₹1000
    • ₹50 flat shipping for orders below ₹1000
  • Cash on Delivery (COD):
    • Additional ₹200 COD handling charges apply
    • COD available on orders up to ₹10,000

Save ₹100 + shipping by choosing prepaid payment!

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Email with tracking number and courier partner details
  • SMS updates on delivery progress
  • Ability to track your order through the courier's website or app
  • Order status updates in your account dashboard

6. Packaging

We take special care in packaging to ensure your Ghee reaches you in perfect condition:

  • Premium glass jars sealed with tamper-proof caps
  • Bubble wrap and protective cushioning
  • Sturdy outer carton boxes
  • Fragile stickers and handling instructions

7. Delivery Attempt

Our courier partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times:

  • Please ensure someone is available to receive the order
  • Valid ID proof may be required for delivery
  • If all attempts fail, the order will be returned to us
  • Return shipping charges may apply for re-delivery

8. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • Please report within 24 hours of delivery (for damaged packages)
  • Share photos of the damaged packaging and product
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund
  • No questions asked for genuine cases

9. Address Changes

You can modify the delivery address:

  • Before the order is dispatched by contacting our support team
  • After dispatch, address changes are subject to courier partner policies
  • Additional charges may apply for address modification after dispatch
