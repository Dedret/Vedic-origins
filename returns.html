Returns & Refunds Policy

Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

100% Quality Guarantee - If you're not satisfied, we'll make it right!

1. Our Quality Commitment

At Vedic Origins, we stand behind the purity and quality of our products. Every jar of Ghee is:

  • Made fresh using traditional Bilona method
  • Lab-tested for purity and quality
  • Packaged with care to ensure freshness
  • Backed by our satisfaction guarantee

2. Return Eligibility

You can return or request a refund if:

  • Product Damaged: Jar arrived broken or Ghee spilled during transit
  • Quality Issue: Product doesn't meet our quality standards (unusual smell, taste, or appearance)
  • Wrong Product: You received a different product than ordered
  • Seal Broken: Tamper-proof seal was broken or damaged before delivery
  • Expired Product: Product received past expiry date (highly unlikely but covered)

3. Return Window

  • Damaged/Wrong Product: Report within 24 hours of delivery
  • Quality Issues: Report within 7 days of delivery
  • Returns requested after this period may not be accepted

4. Non-Returnable Cases

Due to the nature of our food product, we cannot accept returns in these cases:

  • Product seal was opened and used
  • Return request after 7 days of delivery
  • Damage caused by improper storage or handling after delivery
  • Change of mind without any product defect
  • Product stored or used against our guidelines

5. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp (details below)
  2. Provide order number and reason for return
  3. Share clear photos of:
    • Product with seal intact (if applicable)
    • Manufacturing/expiry date on the jar
    • Any visible defects or damage
  4. Our team will review and respond within 24 hours
  5. If approved, we'll arrange pickup or provide return instructions

6. Refund Process

Once your return is approved:

  • Prepaid Orders: Full refund to original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • COD Orders: Refund via bank transfer (provide account details)
  • Shipping charges are refunded for damaged/wrong products
  • You'll receive email confirmation when refund is processed

7. Replacement Option

Instead of a refund, you can opt for:

  • Free replacement of the same product
  • Exchange for different quantity or product
  • Store credit for future purchases (with 10% bonus)
  • Faster processing than refunds (2-3 days)

8. Return Shipping

  • Our Fault: We arrange and pay for return pickup
  • Quality Issues: We provide prepaid return label
  • No return shipping charges for genuine cases
  • Do not ship the product back without our approval

9. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order:

  • Before Dispatch: Full refund, no questions asked
  • After Dispatch: Standard return policy applies
  • Contact us immediately to request cancellation
  • Prepaid cancellations refunded in 3-5 business days

10. Customer Satisfaction

We're committed to your satisfaction:

  • If you're unhappy for any reason, let us know
  • We'll work with you to find a solution
  • Your feedback helps us improve our products and service
  • We value long-term relationships over single transactions

Need Help? Contact us and we'll resolve any issues quickly!
